Recommended Books and Other Stuff for Guitar
 
This page is filled with stuff that I personally own and love. If you see it listed here, it's good stuff.
The Art of Playing Rock Guitar, by Richard Daniels
Richard Daniels' latest book is a welcome extension of the Heavy Guitar Bible. This one goes a lot further in explaining how the guitar fits into music theory (trust me, this stuff is essential and practical knowledge). This book also introduces a number of advanced techniques. Entertaining and filled with amazing pen-and-ink artwork. If you can only buy one guitar book, this is it.
More from Richard Daniels
The Heavy Guitar Bible, by Richard Daniels
As Richard Daniels' first book, this contains all the basic information that he builds on in later books and in his videos. The coverage of musical theory in this book is not as complete as you'll find in his later books, but this will teach you to play, and it is simpler for beginners than his later books.
